AI Assessment Engine

🎤 Speech-to-Text

  • Sarvam AI — tuned for Kannada dialects
  • Dharwad, Mangalurean, North Karnataka variations
  • Handles code-switching (Kannada ↔ Hindi)
  • Fallback: OpenAI Whisper

🧠 LLM Scoring (3 Dimensions)

  • Relevance & Completeness — did they answer?
  • Communication Clarity — is it coherent?
  • Skill Confidence — domain knowledge check
  • Rolled into Assessment Confidence Score (ACS)

🛡️ Proctoring Layer

  • Face liveness & continuous presence monitoring
  • Tab-switch & fullscreen-exit detection
  • Gaze-away & multiple-faces tracking
  • Audio quality & video integrity checks

🔍 Fraud Detection

  • Face embedding comparison (pgvector cosine)
  • Cross-district duplicate matching
  • Device & metadata fingerprinting
  • Behavioural anomaly detection
